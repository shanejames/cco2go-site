Segment 2 · The Failure Pattern

5 Reasons CS AI Projects Fail

1
No Data Foundation
Activity data ≠ behavioral data. Garbage in, garbage out — with a polished interface.
2
Automating Bad Processes
Automation amplifies what it touches. Make sure what it touches is worth amplifying.
3
No Success Metric Defined
"We're using AI" is not a metric. What does success look like in 90 days — in a specific number?
4
Tool Without a Workflow
No documented workflow = tool sits unused within 60 days. Every time.
5
The GPT Wrapper Trap
73% of "AI CS platforms" are a polished UI on a public LLM call. No proprietary model. No CS-specific training. No memory of your customer data.

Segment 5 · The Right Model

Augmentation vs. Replacement

AI can surface that an account is at risk. It cannot replace a CSM who knows that this particular champion is navigating a company restructure and needs a very different conversation than what the risk score suggests.

AI
Handles Routine
Signal monitoring · Health scoring · Data aggregation · Briefing generation · Onboarding tracking
YOU
Handles Complexity
Judgment calls · Relationship dynamics · Negotiation · Strategic pivots · Exception management

The augmentation model is right. The replacement model is wrong. Build for augmentation.
