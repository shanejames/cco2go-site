Why It Doesn't Happen

3 Reasons Discovery
Gets Skipped

REASON 01
Time Pressure
The account is live. Tasks are stacking. The customer wants answers, not questions — so the CSM skips the deep dive.
REASON 02
False Familiarity
The CSM scanned the CRM notes and feels like they know the account. They know the sales narrative. That's different.
REASON 03
Awkward Positioning
Discovery questions feel like going backwards — second-guessing the deal. So the CSM avoids them entirely.

Get These Before You Touch the Account

The 5 Handoff Questions

1
What specific outcome did we promise them? — Exact language. Word for word. That's your renewal benchmark.
2
What was their primary pain point before us? — Your renewal story is always before-and-after. Know the before.
3
Who is the decision-maker vs. the champion? — Who owns outcome, who uses it, who writes the check. Rarely the same person.
4
Were there objections that got papered over? — Unresolved concerns sit in the account. They surface at renewal.
5
What does success look like in 90 days — in the customer's words? — Not sales' words. Not the deck. The customer's mouth.

90-Day Takeover Model

Discover · Design · Drive

Phase 01
DISCOVER
Days 1–30
Understand before you act. Map users, outcomes, and gaps between what sales promised and reality.
Phase 02
DESIGN
Days 31–60
Build the success plan. Prioritize use cases, set milestones, surface misalignments before they cost you.
Phase 03
DRIVE
Days 61–90
Execute. Hit milestones. Show the customer things are happening on purpose — build toward your first EBR.

The Critical Rule

Don't Design Until You've Discovered.
Don't Drive Until You've Designed.

Most CSMs run all three phases simultaneously from day one — and that's why they feel scattered.
By Day 90 you should answer without looking anything up: What did they hire us to do? Are we doing it? What's the proof?

Running Discovery in Practice

Discovery Call:
The 4-Bucket Structure

BUCKET 01
Current State
What's actually happening today with our product in their workflow — not what it should look like, what it looks like right now.
BUCKET 02
Goals & Metrics
Specific, measurable, customer-owned. "Improve our process" is not a goal. "Reduce time-to-close by 15% in Q2" is a goal.
BUCKET 03
Stakeholder Landscape
Users, influencers, and the skeptic. Every account has one. Find them early or they'll find you at renewal.
BUCKET 04
Blockers & Risks
Competing initiative? IT dependency? Legal hold? Surface these in discovery, not when they've delayed your timeline by 60 days.

After Every Discovery Call

Send the Recap
Within 24 Hours

"Here's what I heard. Here's what I'm focused on. Here's what comes next."
Send it to the customer, not just your CRM. If you got something wrong, they'll correct you. Both outcomes are good.
