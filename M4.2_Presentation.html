Segment 2 · Decoding the Exit Story

What They Said vs.
What They Meant

"Budget cuts"
Relationship failure. The money didn't disappear — the value case did. When someone fights for a renewal, they find budget.
"Different direction"
Value gap they never told you about. Either you didn't ask the right questions, or you didn't act on what you heard.
"Chose competitor"
Onboarding or value delivery failure. You didn't get them to value fast enough, and switching looked worth it.
Segment 3 · The Exit Interview

The Autopsy Protocol

This is not a survey. It's a conversation.

WHO RUNS IT Not the CSM who owned the account. Too close. Use a CS leader, executive, or peer.
WHEN Within 48 hours of confirmed churn. The window closes fast. After a week, the memory has been edited.
FORMAT Live conversation. 5 structured questions. Unfiltered. No CRM dropdown options.
Segment 3 · The 5 Exit Interview Questions

Write These Down

  • 1
    When did you first start thinking about leaving?
    The gap between when they started and when they told you = your actual failure window.
  • 2
    Was there a specific moment or event that made it real?
    Find the trigger. Not the root cause — the trigger. You need both.
  • 3
    What could we have done differently in the last 90 days?
    Specific enough that they can actually remember and give you actionable feedback.
  • 4
    Was it a relationship issue, a product issue, or both?
    Most churns are both. The weight matters for where you focus remediation.
  • 5
    Would you consider coming back — and what would it take?
    Not a save play. Intelligence gathering. The answer tells you how far the failure went.
Segment 4 · The Investigation

The 5-Layer Investigation

  • 1
    Surface Story
    The official reason. Starting point — not the answer. Look past it immediately.
  • 2
    Signal Timeline
    When did usage decline? When did support patterns change? Shows when the customer actually started leaving.
  • 3
    Relationship Audit
    Who was the champion? Last direct conversation with an economic buyer? Champion departure with no recovery = late-stage failure.
  • 4
    Onboarding Verdict
    Did they ever reach full value? T2FV vs. benchmark. If 2× benchmark — risk window was open from Day 30.
  • 5
    System Verdict
    Process failure? Individual execution? Tooling gap? Different failures require different fixes — don't confuse them.
