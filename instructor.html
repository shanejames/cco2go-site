Session Delivery Guide

Pre-sessionReview the teleprompter script 24 hrs ahead. Note the "Do This Monday" actions — these drive week-to-week continuity.
OpeningEvery module has a cold open. Don't skip it — it's designed to create immediate tension before the framework arrives.
Q&AAllow 5 min post-module. Save "how does this apply to my company" questions for end of session.
AsyncUse video scripts for recorded cohort content. They're cut differently — scene directions and pacing markers included.

Cohort Session Rhythm

Phase 1Weeks 1–3: 3 modules/week, ~35 min content + 15 min debrief. Focus on dismantling assumptions before building new ones.
Phase 2Weeks 4–6: Heavy application. Assign the "Do This Monday" actions as live deliverables due before next session.
Phase 3Weeks 7–9: Combined sections. Build toward the Week 9 Manifesto — students should arrive with a draft.
